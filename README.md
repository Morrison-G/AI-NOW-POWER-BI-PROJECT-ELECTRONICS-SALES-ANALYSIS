# AI-NOW-POWER-BI-PROJECT-ELECTRONICS-SALES-ANALYSIS
> This Power BI work on Electronics sales case study aims to ETL the data, create a Date table using DAX and do Data Modelling, creating a report with different visuals analyzing the Sales data by different dimensions like Channel, Product Category, and Zone. also showing the Profits and Profits Margin over the year and other insight that aids business decision making.
> Data Analysed by Morrison

## Project Overview
> This case study analyses Electronics sales data creating a date table to aid in modeling and visual creation of insight that aid business decision making.

## Tool Used
> Power BI Desktop (power query, Power BI Tool, power-point for background charts creation, visual charts in power BI).

## Data Cleaning Actions
> Some data cleaning steps were applied to make the dataset proper for analysis without errors, which was promoted headers and change data type.

## Key Business Questions Answered
1.	Creation of a Date table using DAX and Data Modelling.
2.	 Time Intelligence Measures Created on a separate Measure table
 a. Sales Year To Date 
b. Sales Quarter To Date
 c. Same Period Last Year
 d. Sales Year On Year Variance Sales Last Month 
e. Sales Year On Year Variance % (format to percentage)

## Findings
> 1.	Total sales was 56.25m.
> 2.	Total cost was 23.80m.
> 3.	Profit was 32.45m.
> 4.	Profit margin was o.58%.
> 5.	Sales Year On Year was 16.0m.
> 6.	Highest sale by channel was from stores at 480 different products.
> 7.	Highest sales by products was computers at 21.63m.
> 8.	Highest sales by state was ebonyi state at 12.7m.
